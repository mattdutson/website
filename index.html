Summary

I am a computer science PhD student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am advised by Mohit Gupta. Broadly, my research interests include computer vision, computer graphics, and machine learning. My current research is in single-photon sensing and event-based neural networks.

I believe that computing has enormous potential to improve the human condition. Unfortunately, technology can also concentrate power and threaten democratic institutions. I strive to make my research consistent with principles of democracy, privacy, and human dignity.
